Our Process

PROCESS

Our Simple 4-Step Process

Step 1

Contact Us

Reach out to us by filling out our quick online form or giving us a call. We’ll gather basic information about your property to start the process. No hassle, no long paperwork—just a simple way to begin.

Step 2

Get Your Cash Offer

Within 24 hours of contacting us, we’ll evaluate your property and present you with a fair, no-obligation cash offer. We use local market data and the condition of your home to give you the best possible price.


Step 3

Accept the Offer

Once you’ve reviewed the offer, you’re free to accept it with no pressure. We’ll work with you to schedule a closing date that suits your timeline, whether you need to move quickly or prefer to take your time.

Step 4

Close and Get Paid

On closing day, we handle all the paperwork and final details. Once everything is signed, you’ll receive your cash payment. It’s fast, simple, and stress-free.
